Welcome to ReadUp!

A free reading & spelling app for kids in Pre-K through 3rd grade.

Built by Transforming Youth, Inc. to help every child become a confident reader and speller.

Here's what your child can do:

✨ AI Stories — Brand new stories every time, on 18+ topics

📸 Upload a Passage — Snap a photo of any page and read along

📝 Spelling Words — Upload a list, study, practice, and take quizzes

📖 Read Together — Read with a friend from any device

🔊 Word-by-Word Tracking — Words highlight as they're read aloud

🔁
The 3-Read Method

Research shows that reading a story 3 times is one of the fastest ways to build reading fluency.

▶ Read #1 — Listen & follow along

The teacher reads while words light up yellow. Your child sees and hears every word.

▶ Read #2 — Read together

Your child reads along with the teacher voice. Familiar words come faster.

▶ Read #3 — Read on your own

Your child reads independently and records their own reading speed!

This works for AI-generated stories and pages you upload from books or worksheets. Tap any word to hear it and learn what it means!

📝
Spelling & Study Tools

ReadUp! isn't just for reading — it's a complete study companion!

📸 Upload Spelling Words

Take a photo of any spelling list. ReadUp! reads the words and creates definitions automatically.

📖 Study Mode

Full-screen view of every word with syllables and meanings. Tap to hear them spoken.

🎯 Practice & Quiz

Multiple choice practice, then a real spelling quiz where your child types each word from scratch.

📖 Reading Mode

Full-screen reading with play/pause controls — great for reading along on a big screen.

👤
For Parents & Teachers

ReadUp! tracks progress so you can see how your child is growing.

📊 Parent Dashboard

See stories read, time spent, favorite topics, and words your child needed help with.

🔒 PIN Protected

Default PIN is 1234. Change it anytime in the dashboard.

🏠 Easy Navigation

The Home button is always at the bottom. Your child can get back anytime.
