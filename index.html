üìä Text Statistics

After uploading or pasting text, you'll see:

  • Paragraph Count - How many sections
  • Estimated Time - How long it will take (adjusts with speed)

Adjust the slider to change reading speed:

üî§ Font Size Options

Choose the best size for your child:

A purple "üîÅ Repeat" button appears on each paragraph while it's being read. Click it to hear that paragraph again - perfect for comprehension!

üí° Tips for Success

üì∏ For Best OCR Results:

  • Take clear, well-lit photos
  • Keep text straight (not at an angle)
  • Works best with printed text

üéØ Reading Practice:

  • Start with shorter texts (100-200 words)
  • Use slower speed for new or difficult material
  • Double-click unfamiliar words to save them
  • Review saved words after each session

‚è±Ô∏è Session Length:

  • Keep sessions 10-15 minutes for younger children
  • Use the estimated time to plan your session
  • Take breaks between longer readings

üîß Troubleshooting

Voice sounds robotic or wrong?

  • This app works best in Chrome or Safari
  • Some browsers have limited voice options
  • Try refreshing the page

Image upload not working?

  • Make sure the photo is clear and well-lit
  • Try a different image format
  • As a backup, you can always paste text manually

App won't start reading?

  • Make sure text is in the box
  • Check that browser isn't muted
  • Try refreshing the page
