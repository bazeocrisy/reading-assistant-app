What is Words Per Minute (WPM)?

WPM measures reading speed. Your child read out loud for 60 seconds, then tapped the last word they reached. The number of words read IS the WPM score.

The math: words in 60 sec = WPM

How schools measure this:

ReadUp uses the same 60-second method as school assessments like DIBELS, AIMSweb, and AMIRA. Schools call this Oral Reading Fluency (ORF) or WCPM (Words Correct Per Minute).

Note: School assessments also subtract errors (mispronounced words). ReadUp counts total words reached — for official accuracy scoring, use your school's assessment tool.
