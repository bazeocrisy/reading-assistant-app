## Files You Need to Upload

You need to upload TWO files to your GitHub repository:

1. **index.html** - The updated Reading Assistant app (with new Help button)
2. **parent-guide.html** - The beautiful parent guide

## How to Upload to GitHub

### Step 1: Go to Your Repository
Visit: https://github.com/bazeocrisy/reading-assistant-app

### Step 2: Upload the Updated index.html
1. Click on the existing **"index.html"** file
2. Click the **pencil icon** (Edit) in the top right
3. Delete all the old content
4. Open the new **index.html** from your computer
5. Copy ALL the content
6. Paste it into GitHub
7. Scroll down and click **"Commit changes"**

### Step 3: Upload parent-guide.html
1. Go back to the main repository page
2. Click **"Add file"** → **"Upload files"**
3. Drag and drop **parent-guide.html** from your computer
4. Click **"Commit changes"**

### Step 4: Wait 1-2 Minutes
GitHub will automatically update your live site!
