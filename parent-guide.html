Upload Screenshot to ChatGPT

Go to chatgpt.com in your web browser
Sign in (or create a free account if you don't have one)
Look for the paperclip icon üìé or Attach button
Click it and upload your screenshot
Type this message: "Please extract all the text from this image as raw text"

üí° TIP: ChatGPT is very accurate at reading text from images. It works with printed text, handwriting, and even curved text in photos!

3
Copy the Text from ChatGPT

ChatGPT will show you all the text it found in your image
Click and drag to select all the text (or press Ctrl+A / Command+A)
Right-click and choose "Copy"
OR press Ctrl+C (Windows) or Command+C (Mac)

Open the Reading Assistant app in your browser:

Right-click and choose "Paste" (or press Ctrl+V / Command+V)
Click the "Start Reading" button
